This website is for "San Francisco information center."

SFID is based in San Francisco on a privae web potal. This site suggests various information promote convenient travel to and from San Francisco. San Francisco is in northern California. San Francisco is one of the most populous cities in the United States. The city is surrounding a by what is known as the San Francisco Bay Area, and is part of the larger San Jose, San Francisco and Oakland United analytical area. As a famous tourist destination, San Francisco is known or its fog, cool summers, hills, architecture, and landmarks such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Cable cars, Alcatraz, Fisherman's Wharf, Chinatown, and the Heart statue. Also, there are many parks and beaches that belong to San Francisco. Most parks in San Francisco are located near by the Golden Gate Area. Both are two most ocean beach, which is famous for surfing, and baker beach are within Golden Gate Area too. Dolores park and Alamo square are two of the most famous parks in San Francisco.
