How It Works

  2. Set up in your mobile phone.
  3. See where you had it If you use the Bluetooth during the whole day, it tracks last time automatically.
  4. Click the ring button If you can’t still find, just touch the ring button on the screen. If your tile is located within the 100 foot Bluetooth range, it will played a melody until you find it. If you lose your phone, just press the any tile simply that you already set
