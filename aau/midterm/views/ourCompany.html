About Tile

Tile is an app that helps people never lose anything again. For me, I used to lose something before leaving home. I spent the unnecessary time because I wasted time for looking something such as house key, wallet, bag, laptop even cell phone too. The tile has tracker function by using Bluetooth that connected with phone, Bluetooth will be keep track your items.
