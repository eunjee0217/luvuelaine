SPQR

SPQR is a Northern California restaurant inspired by Italian cuisine and wine.

An acronym for Senatus Populusque Romanus.

SPQR translates to “The People and Senate of Rome” and was the emblem of the Roman Empire.

Led by Chef Matthew Accarrino a Food & Wine “Best New Chef 2014" and celebrated Sommelier/Owner Shelley Lindgren.

A proud recipient of a Michelin star for both 2013 and 2014.

we strive for balance in offering the highest quality of food, wine and service for

each guest’s experience in a intimate and inviting atmosphere.
